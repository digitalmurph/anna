The maker

Hi, I’m Anna.

Some of you may know me as ‘Carmelite Anna’ from Twitter — now known as X!

There is a reason I have Carmelite in my handle. For 6.5 years I lived my life as a Carmelite nun tucked away in a monastery in York with 20 other likeminded women. It was a challenging life but one that led me deep into the Heart of Jesus and nurtured my faith in Him.

Sadly at the age of 25, after six and a half years and before solemn profession, it became obvious I had to leave due to ill health. This was one of the hardest times in my life — but even now, over 30 years later...
