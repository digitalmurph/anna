## about page
### Hi, I'm Anna.

Some of you may know me as 'Carmelite Anna' from Twitter now known as X!

There is a reason I have Carmelite in my handle. For 6.5 years I lived my life as a Carmelite nun tucked away in a monastery in York with 20 other likeminded women. 

It was a challenging life but one that led me deep into the Heart of Jesus and nurtured my faith in Him. 
Sadly at the age of 25 after six and a half years and before solemn profession ,it became obvious I had to leave due to ill health. 

This was one of the hardest times in my life but even now over 30 years later I still have the Carmelite devotion deep in my heart!

Now I’m married with 3 adult children and several years ago after a difficult time when I felt my faith was drifting away I started making rosaries. I still remember the elation I felt on that Sunday morning when I finished my first wire wrapped bead after hours of practice !

Then I got the rosary making bug! I made rosaries for anyone who would have them until it became impossible to fund all this.

I started selling them on Etsy and then here on my website. I still give rosaries away - look out for my giveaway every first Friday on X where

 You have the opportunity to win one. I’ve sent rosaries to the missions too and support an orphanage in Uganda . The sale of my rosaries helps me to continue this.
 
I hope you enjoy browsing through my rosaries. If they’ve sold out just contact me and I’ll most likely be able to make it again for you.

Thanks for reading!
God bless 
Anna

I also love custom orders. You bring the intention; I'll find the perfect beads to match it.
